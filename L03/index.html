Delicious Pizza Recipe

Did you know that the first modern pizza was created in Naples, Italy? It's called the Margherita pizza, named after the Queen of Italy, and has tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil on it.

Fun Fact: The world's most expensive pizza costs $12,000 and is topped with white truffles and 24-karat gold!

Tasty Tacos

Tacos are a traditional Mexican dish made with a crispy or soft tortilla shell filled with various ingredients like meat, cheese, lettuce, and salsa.

Fun Fact: The world's largest taco was made in Mexico in 2003 and measured over 246 feet long!
