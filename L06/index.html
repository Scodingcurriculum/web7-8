Yoga: Margin and Padding Concepts

Understanding Margin in Yoga

This yoga mat has a margin of 20 pixels.

In yoga, margin represents the personal space around your mat, providing a comfortable area for your practice.

Understanding Padding in Yoga

This yoga mat has a padding of 20 pixels.

Padding in yoga represents the space within your mat, allowing you to move freely and safely during your practice.

Combining Margin and Padding in Yoga

This yoga mat has both margin and padding applied.

By combining margin and padding, you can create the perfect space for your yoga practice, ensuring a harmonious and balanced experience.
